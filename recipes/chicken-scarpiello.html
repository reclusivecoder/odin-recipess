Chicken Scarpariello

Description

One of my favorite go-to spicy chicken dishes to warm you up this holiday season!

Ingredients (courtesy of Jim M @allrecipes)

  • 1 1/4 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 white wine
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper

Steps (courtesy of Jim M @allrecipes

  1. Cut chicken into 1x3-inch strips; dredge in flour.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat. Add chicken; cook, turning occasionally, until lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove chicken with tongs; keep warm.
  3. Add shallots and garlic to skillet; saute until softened, about 1 minute.
  4. Stir water, wine, bouillon cube, rosemary, salt, and pepper into skillet; cook, stirring frequently, until liquid is reduced by half, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Return chicken to skillet; cook until sauce is thickened and chicken is heated through, about 2 to 3 minutes.
